Two rules the gate never breaks

  • Stakes can pull one item up. Any destructive/irreversible op (deletes, payments, prod writes) makes guardrails required — even in a prototype.
  • It never invents a requirement you don't have. No LLM/fuzzy parts → rows 4 & 6 drop off automatically. No nagging.

Verdict

GO — all required (●) items checked.
GO-WITH-GAPS — required met, recommended (◒) gaps remain.
NOT-READY — a required (●) item is missing.
